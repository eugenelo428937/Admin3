info Design Principles

Atmospheric Design System

  • Muted Complementary Colors: Warm (terracotta), Rose, Teal, Slate accents
  • Large Organic Curves: Soft-edged circular shapes with heavy blur (30-60px)
  • Overlapping Translucent Layers: Multiple elements at varying opacities (0.3-0.5)
  • Glassmorphism: Backdrop-blur on avatars, labels, and decorative shapes
  • Rounded Corners: 16-20px radius on cards and elements
  • Low Contrast: Atmospheric, misty feel with soft edges

Three Variations

  1. Organic: Large flowing curves, warm corner accent, four-stop gradient, heavy blur
  2. Glass: Multiple glass shapes with backdrop-blur, decorative overlay, translucent layers
  3. Organic Glass (Combined): Best of both - flowing organic curves + glass shapes + overlay + avatar glow
